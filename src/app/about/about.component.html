About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Pankti shah and I am a professional makeup artist and hairstylist with 11years of experience in the beauty industry. I have developed my skills by learning from my mother and has since honed my craft to become a professional makeup artist. I am passionate about enhancing the natural beauty of my clients and creating stunning looks that make them feel confident and beautiful. I have worked with numerous clients, including brides, models, and everyday individuals.

My philosophy is to use high-quality products and techniques to enhance each client's unique features and bring out their individual beauty. I specialize in creating flawless makeup looks, elegant updos, trendy hairstyles, and everything in between.
